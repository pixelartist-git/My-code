About Us

Health Guardian is committed to tackling the growing threat of non-communicable diseases (NCDs). Through prevention, early detection, and effective treatments, we aim to create a healthier future for all by promoting healthy lifestyles, supporting research, and advocating for policy changes.

Our Goals

  • Support policies that promote healthy lifestyles and reduce risk factors for NCDs.
  • Educate communities on causes, symptoms, and prevention of NCDs.
  • Support research to develop treatments and prevention strategies for NCDs.
  • Partner with individuals, organizations, and schools for education and prevention.

Our Background

Founded in 2024, Health Guardian began as a small initiative educating communities about NCDs. Our team of students, researchers, and volunteers is dedicated to empowering individuals with knowledge and resources to lead healthier lives.
