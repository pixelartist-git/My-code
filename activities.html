Activities

Health Guardian is dedicated to improving global health by addressing the growing threat of non-communicable diseases (NCDs) such as heart disease, stroke, cancer, diabetes, and chronic lung diseases, which are the leading causes of death worldwide. We believe our activities will contribute to a healthier future by promoting healthy lifestyles, advocating for policy change, supporting innovative research, and building strong partnerships.

For the past weeks, we have been focusing on activities related to NCDs, including interviews, creating posters, making videos, and writing blogs.

Recent Activities

We've been actively working on raising awareness about NCDs and encouraging healthy living. Here are some of our recent efforts:

  • Conducted interviews with healthcare professionals.
  • Created and distributed informative posters.
  • Produced a video series on the impact of NCDs.
  • Published a blog on the importance of lifestyle changes.
